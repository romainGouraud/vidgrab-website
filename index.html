FAQ

Common questions

Does it work on YouTube? +

No. Chrome Web Store policy bans YouTube downloaders, so VidGrab won't ever target YouTube. We focus on platforms where downloading your own content is legitimate: Vimeo, Bunny.net, HLS streams, MP4 directs.

Does it bypass DRM? +

Never. VidGrab respects all DRM (Widevine, FairPlay, PlayReady) and platform protections. It only downloads videos that are technically downloadable from your browser already — it just makes it one click instead of fifteen.

Is my data safe? +

Yes. Everything runs locally in your browser. Zero tracking, zero analytics. The only network call we make is to verify your license with our payment provider (Stripe via ExtensionPay). See our privacy policy for details.

What's actually included in the Free plan? +

3 days of unlimited downloads at install (no credit card needed), then 5 free downloads total. After that, you'll need Pro for unlimited access. The free plan has no feature limits — you get the same engine, just a quota.

Does it work on Mac, Windows, Linux? +

Yes — anywhere Chrome (or Edge, Brave, any Chromium browser) runs. No native app required.

Do I need to create an account? +

Nope. Free plan is fully anonymous. For Pro, your license is tied to your purchase email — you can restore it on any device just by entering that email. No password, no signup form.
